# LSBU-Glucose-App
A Matlab Demo App for analysing blood glucose by using Near Infrared Spectroscopy
## Step 1. Clone or Download the project and save it into your local computer
![Clone or Download](https://github.com/PerryXiao2015/LSBU-Glucose-App/blob/main/images/Picture0.png?raw=true)
## Step 2. Locate the LSBUGlucoseApp.mlappinstall file, double click to install it as a Matlab App. You will need Matlab 2020b or newer.
![Install the Matlab App](https://github.com/PerryXiao2015/LSBU-Glucose-App/blob/main/images/Picture1.png?raw=true)
## Step 3. From Matlab App tab, locate the LSBUGlucoseApp, double click to run it.
![Run Matlab App](https://github.com/PerryXiao2015/LSBU-Glucose-App/blob/main/images/Picture2.png?raw=true)
## Step 4. From LSBUGlucoseApp, choose "Train" tab on the right panel, then click "Load CSV" button to load an experiment configuration file, such as Experiment1.csv (arm), Experiment1-finger.csv (finger), or Experiment2.csv (sugar in water).
![Load Experiment Data](https://github.com/PerryXiao2015/LSBU-Glucose-App/blob/main/images/Picture3.png?raw=true)
![Load Experiment Data](https://github.com/PerryXiao2015/LSBU-Glucose-App/blob/main/images/Picture4.png?raw=true)
## Step 5. From LSBUGlucoseApp, click "Train" button to Train the model.
![Train the Model](https://github.com/PerryXiao2015/LSBU-Glucose-App/blob/main/images/Picture5.png?raw=true)
![Train the Model](https://github.com/PerryXiao2015/LSBU-Glucose-App/blob/main/images/Picture6.png?raw=true)
## Step 6. From LSBUGlucoseApp, choose the "Detect" tab on the right panel, click "Select a NIR File" button to predict the blood glucose level.
![Run Matlab App](https://github.com/PerryXiao2015/LSBU-Glucose-App/blob/main/images/Picture7.png?raw=true)
